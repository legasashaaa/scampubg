FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is this the official PUBG Mobile website?

Yes, this is an official partner site. We are official partners of Tencent Games and operate within the PUBG Mobile Partner Program. Our activities are fully authorized by the developers.

Will the prize definitely come to my account?

Yes, 100% guarantee of receiving the prize. All winnings are sent automatically within 24 hours after confirming the data. The system is integrated with the official PUBG Mobile API, which guarantees delivery of prizes to the specified account.

Why do I need to log into a social network?

Logging into a social network is necessary for verifying your identity and preventing fraud. This allows us to ensure that a real person, not a bot, receives the prize. All data is protected and not shared with third parties.

What if I don't specify the account ID?

Without specifying the PUBG Mobile account ID, we cannot send you the prize. This is a mandatory field, as it is by this identifier that the system determines which game account to credit the won items to.

What to do if the prize doesn't arrive within 24 hours?

In rare cases with high server load, processing may take up to 48 hours. If the prize has not been credited to your account within this time, please contact our technical support with your ID and winning time.

Is it safe to enter social network credentials?

Yes, it is absolutely safe. We use secure SSL encryption and do not store your passwords. Authorization occurs through the official APIs of social networks, similar to how you log into applications on your phone.

Can I win more than once?

Each user can participate in the promotion once per day. This is done to give as many players as possible the chance to win. Come back tomorrow to try your luck again!

What prizes can be won?

Our roulette features exclusive items from PUBG Mobile: rare skins, weapons, vehicles, equipment, and in-game UC currency. All prizes are official game content and do not violate the user agreement.
